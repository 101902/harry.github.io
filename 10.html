System Goals

  • Centralized student information storage for easy access.
  • Optimize course scheduling to meet student preferences.
  • Automated marketing to enhance enrollment and retention.
  • Data-driven decision-making through analytics.

Core Modules

1. Student Management Module

  • Data Collection: Name, contact details, experience, health status.
  • Preferences: Course types, schedules, and coach preferences.
  • Classification: Beginner, intermediate, advanced levels.

2. Course Management Module

  • Schedule Management: Calendar view for coach and venue availability.
  • Personalized Recommendations: Match courses to student needs.

3. Marketing Automation Module

  • Email Campaigns: Welcome emails, reminders, and reactivation messages.
  • Referral Programs: Incentivize students for bringing in new members.

4. Data Analytics Module

  • Track Activity: Attendance and participation trends.
  • Feedback Collection: Satisfaction surveys for continuous improvement.
  • Generate Reports: Monthly statistics on enrollment and retention.

Implementation Steps

  • Select CRM platform or develop custom solution.
  • Prepare and import student data, ensuring accuracy.
  • Test all modules and refine based on feedback.
  • Launch the system and train staff for seamless adoption.

Future Development

  • Mobile app integration for easier student access.
  • Online course support for remote learning options.
  • AI-powered recommendations for enhanced personalization.
