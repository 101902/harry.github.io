Feature Modules

1. Student Information Management Module

  • Data Collection: Name, gender, age, contact information.
  • Learning Background: Badminton experience and goals.
  • Health Status: Injury history or restrictions.
  • Preference Settings: Preferred class times, coach type, and learning style.
  • Classification: Level (beginner, intermediate, advanced) and needs (entertainment, progression, competition).

2. Course Arrangement Module

  • Course Matching: Recommend suitable courses based on data.
  • Time Management: Avoid scheduling conflicts (coach and venue).

3. Marketing and Promotion Module

  • Automated Campaigns: Welcome emails, tips, and reactivation messages.
  • Referral Program: Reward students for bringing in new members.

4. Data Analysis Module

  • Activity Tracking: Analyze attendance and preferred times.
  • Satisfaction Surveys: Collect feedback on coaches and course quality.
  • Business Insights: Generate monthly reports on metrics like new students and retention rates.

Implementation Steps

  • System Setup: Choose a CRM tool or custom development.
  • Data Import and Cleaning: Ensure accurate and categorized information.
  • Testing and Optimization: Adjust based on feedback.
  • Launch and Training: Train staff and officially deploy the system.

Future Development Directions

  • Mobile app integration for better accessibility.
  • Online payment features for seamless registration.
  • AI analytics for more precise course recommendations.
